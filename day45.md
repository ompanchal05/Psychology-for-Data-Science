# 🙋 Day 45 — HR Interview + Self Introduction Preparation

## 🎯 Goal
Prepare for HR round questions and build confident self-introduction.

---

## 📌 Most Common HR Questions

1. Tell me about yourself
2. Why should we hire you?
3. What are your strengths?
4. What are your weaknesses?
5. Why data science?
6. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
7. Why do you want this internship/job?

---

## 🎤 1. Tell Me About Yourself (Short Answer)

“My name is Om Panchal and I am currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science.

I have a strong interest in Data Science, Machine Learning, and user behavior analysis.

Recently, I completed projects in behavioral churn prediction and recommendation systems where I combined psychology with machine learning.

I enjoy solving real-world problems using data and I am looking for an opportunity to grow my practical skills.”

---

## 💪 2. Strengths

- Quick learner
- Analytical thinking
- Curious mindset
- Consistent worker
- Good problem-solving

---

## ⚠️ 3. Weaknesses

“Sometimes I spend extra time making my work more accurate, but I am learning to balance speed with efficiency.”

---

## 🎯 4. Why Should We Hire You?

“I bring both technical understanding and business thinking. I focus not only on building models but also on understanding user behavior and generating useful insights.”

---

## 🚀 5. Why Data Science?

“I enjoy finding patterns in data and using those patterns to solve business problems. Data science gives me the opportunity to combine logic, analysis, and real impact.”

---

## 🧠 Practice Task

- Speak self introduction 5 times
- Practice strengths/weakness answer
- Maintain smile and confidence

---

## 🏁 Outcome

✔ HR questions prepared  
✔ Self introduction ready  
✔ Better speaking confidence  

---

## 💬 Summary

Good HR answers should sound natural, honest, and confident.
