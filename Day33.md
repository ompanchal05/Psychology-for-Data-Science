# 🧠 What is a Recommendation System?

A recommendation system:

> Suggests items to users based on behavior, preferences, and patterns.

Examples:
- Netflix → movies
- Amazon → products
- YouTube → videos

---

# 🧠 Types of Recommendation Systems

---

## 1️⃣ Content-Based Filtering

Recommends based on:
- What user liked before  

Example:
User watches action movies → show more action movies  

---

## 2️⃣ Collaborative Filtering

Recommends based on:
- Similar users  

Example:
Users like you also liked this  

---

## 3️⃣ Hybrid System

Combination of both  
(Used in real companies)

---

# 🔥 Psychology Behind Recommendations

---

## 1️⃣ Confirmation Bias

Users prefer:
- Content similar to past behavior  

👉 System shows similar items  

---

## 2️⃣ Habit Formation

Repeated content builds habit  

👉 Same type of content → retention  

---

## 3️⃣ Dopamine & Reward

Fast, engaging content → repeated use  

👉 Reels, shorts, suggestions  

---

## 4️⃣ Novelty vs Familiarity

Balance:

- Familiar → comfort  
- New → excitement  

👉 Best systems mix both  

---

## 5️⃣ Attention Optimization

System tracks:
- Watch time  
- Click behavior  
- Scroll speed  

---
