# 📈 Day 48 — KPI & Business Metrics for Data Science

## 🎯 Goal
Understand important business metrics used in real companies.

---

## 📌 What are KPIs?

KPI = Key Performance Indicator

These metrics measure:
- growth
- engagement
- retention
- business performance

---

## 📊 Important Metrics

### 1. Retention Rate
How many users continue using the product

### 2. Churn Rate
How many users leave the product

### 3. CTR (Click Through Rate)
How often users click

### 4. Conversion Rate
Users completing a target action

### 5. DAU / MAU
Daily and monthly active users

### 6. Session Time
How long users stay active

---

## 🧠 Why Metrics Matter

Metrics help companies:
- improve products
- increase engagement
- reduce churn
- grow revenue

---

## 💡 Example

Observation:
Low retention rate

Meaning:
Users are not finding enough value

Possible Action:
Improve onboarding and recommendations
