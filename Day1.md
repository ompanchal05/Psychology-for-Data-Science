Psychology is the **scientific study of the mind and behavior.**

- **Mind** = thoughts, feelings, memories, beliefs, decisions.
- **Behavior** = actions, reactions, habits, emotions, expressions.

Psychology helps us understand:
- Why people think differently
- Why people make certain decisions
- Why emotions affect actions
- Why behavior changes based on environment
