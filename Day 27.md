---

## 🔍 Key Evaluation Insights

### 1️⃣ Accuracy is NOT enough

A model can be accurate but still:

- Miss churn users  
- Misclassify critical cases  

👉 Focus on **Recall (churn detection)**

---

### 2️⃣ False Negatives are dangerous

If churn is predicted as “stay”:
→ Business loses customer  

---

### 3️⃣ Precision vs Recall Trade-off

- High Recall → Catch more churn users  
- High Precision → Fewer false alerts  

👉 Choose based on business need

---

## 🧠 Behavioral Insights (MOST IMPORTANT)

---

### 🔹 Insight 1 — Habit Strength

Observation:
Low tenure → High churn

Psychology:
Users haven’t formed a habit yet.

Action:
- Improve onboarding
- Encourage early engagement

---

### 🔹 Insight 2 — Frustration

Observation:
High support calls → High churn

Psychology:
Negative experiences reduce motivation.

Action:
- Improve customer support
- Fix pain points

---

### 🔹 Insight 3 — Perceived Value

Observation:
High cost → High churn

Psychology:
Users feel they are not getting value.

Action:
- Improve value perception
- Offer better pricing or features

---

### 🔹 Insight 4 — Engagement

Observation:
Low usage → High churn

Psychology:
Lack of motivation & habit break.

Action:
- Personalized recommendations
- Re-engagement campaigns

---

## 📊 Behavioral Segmentation

Segment users into groups:

| Segment | Behavior | Strategy |
|--------|----------|----------|
| New Users | Low habit | Strong onboarding |
| Active Users | High engagement | Maintain value |
| At-Risk Users | Declining usage | Intervention |
| Frustrated Users | High complaints | Improve UX |

---

## 🤖 From Model → Business Strategy

| Data Insight | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| Low engagement | Send personalized reminders |
| High complaints | Improve service quality |
| High cost sensitivity | Offer discounts |
| New users churn | Build habit loop early |
