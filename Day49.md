# 🚀 Day 49 — End-to-End Data Science Workflow

## 🎯 Goal
Understand the complete workflow of a real-world data science project.

---

## 📌 Step 1 — Problem Understanding

Understand:
- business problem
- user issue
- project goal

Example:
Why are users churning?

---

## 📊 Step 2 — Data Collection

Collect data from:
- databases
- CSV files
- APIs
- user activity logs

---

## 🧹 Step 3 — Data Cleaning

Handle:
- missing values
- duplicates
- wrong formats
- outliers

---

## 🔍 Step 4 — Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analyze:
- distributions
- patterns
- correlations
- trends

---

## 🧠 Step 5 — Feature Engineering

Create meaningful features.

Example:
- engagement score
- habit strength
- frustration level

---

## 🤖 Step 6 — Model Building

Train ML models:
- Logistic Regression
- Random Forest
- XGBoost

---

## 📈 Step 7 — Evaluation

Use metrics:
- accuracy
- precision
- recall
- F1 score

---

## 📊 Step 8 — Visualization

Create:
- charts
- dashboards
- reports

---

## 💼 Step 9 — Business Insights

Convert results into:
- recommendations
- product improvements
- business actions

---

## 🚀 Step 10 — Deployment (Basic Idea)

Model is used in:
- apps
- websites
- dashboards
- prediction systems

---

## 🏁 Outcome

✔ Understand full workflow  
✔ Ready for real projects  
✔ Better interview preparation  

---

## 💬 Summary

Data science is not only about models — it is a complete problem-solving process.
