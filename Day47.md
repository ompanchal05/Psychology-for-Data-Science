# 🧠 Day 47 — Product Thinking for Data Science

## 🎯 Goal
Learn how data scientists think about products, users, and business impact.

---

## 📌 What is Product Thinking?

Product thinking means:

- Understanding user problems
- Improving user experience
- Using data for business decisions

---

## 🧠 Important Questions

1. Why are users leaving?
2. Which feature increases engagement?
3. What improves retention?
4. How can user experience improve?

---

## 📊 Important Metrics

- DAU / MAU
- Retention Rate
- Churn Rate
- CTR
- Session Time

---

## 🤖 Data Scientist Role

- Analyze user behavior
- Find patterns
- Suggest improvements
- Support product decisions

---

## 💡 Example

Observation:
Users leave after onboarding

Interpretation:
Onboarding may be confusing

Action:
Simplify onboarding flow

---

## 🚀 Product Thinking Mindset

Always ask:

- What problem is being solved?
- What does the user feel?
- What business impact exists?

---

## 🏁 Outcome

✔ Understand product thinking  
✔ Better business understanding  
✔ Improved interview readiness  

---

## 💬 Summary

Good data scientists understand both data and product behavior.
