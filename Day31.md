# 🔥 Mock Interview Questions (Practice These)

---

## 🧩 Q1: Why are users churning?

### ❌ Weak Answer:
“Maybe price is high.”

### ✅ Strong Answer:
“Churn usually happens due to behavioral reasons like loss of motivation, frustration, or broken habit loops.

From a data perspective, I would check:
- Decline in engagement
- Increase in complaints
- Usage patterns

Psychologically, low engagement indicates habit break, and high complaints indicate frustration.

I would segment users and build a churn prediction model to identify at-risk users and suggest targeted interventions.”

---

## 🧩 Q2: Why did engagement drop?

### ✅ Strong Answer:

“Engagement drop can be due to:

- Loss of novelty  
- Cognitive overload  
- Poor content relevance  

From psychology, users need stimulation and value.

From data:
- Session time  
- Scroll behavior  
- Drop-off rates  

Solution:
- Improve personalization  
- Reduce overload  
- Introduce controlled novelty”

---

## 🧩 Q3: A/B test improved CTR but reduced retention — why?

### ✅ Strong Answer:

“This suggests a psychological mismatch.

Users clicked due to:
- curiosity  
- clickbait  
- novelty  

But did not stay because:
- expectation mismatch  
- low value  

This is a classic case where short-term metrics improved but long-term trust decreased.”

---

## 🧩 Q4: How would you design a recommendation system?

### ✅ Strong Answer:

“I would balance:

- Exploitation → what user likes  
- Exploration → new content  

Psychology:
- Avoid boredom  
- Maintain curiosity  
- Use familiarity + novelty  

Data:
- Past behavior  
- Session patterns  
- Engagement signals  

Goal:
- Long-term retention, not just clicks”

---

## 🧩 Q5: How do you make ML ethical?

### ✅ Strong Answer:

“I ensure:

- Fairness → no bias  
- Transparency → explain decisions  
- Privacy → protect user data  
- Non-manipulation → avoid exploiting psychology  

Because ML decisions affect real people.”
